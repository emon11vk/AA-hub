# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
Primary: Accounting & Auditing students practicing bookkeeping operations.
Secondary: Instructors who set exercises, grade work, and cross-check results.
Context: Academic learning environment, focusing on understanding the flow of accounting entries and reports.

## Product Purpose
A minimalized enterprise accounting web app (streamlined from MISA SME). It provides a simulated environment for students to practice the full cycle of accounting: recording transactions, posting to ledger, and preparing foundational financial reports. Success means 100% correct ledger balancing and fully automated report generation from vouchers in ≤ 5 actions.

## Positioning
The essential core of enterprise accounting software, stripped of distracting modules to make the cause-and-effect of double-entry bookkeeping immediately visible to learners.

## Brand Personality
Educational, precise, professional, focused, and accessible.

## Anti-references
Full commercial ERPs (like the complete MISA SME) that are visually complex, cluttered with irrelevant modules, and have a steep learning curve.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over feature density**: Keep the interface clean and focus only on the core cash/bank transaction flow.
- **Cause and effect visibility**: The relationship between a journal entry and its downstream reports must be immediately apparent.
- **Data accuracy above all**: Emphasize balance checks, control warnings, and accurate numerical representation (VN thousands separator).
- **Standardized but un-intimidating**: Use official Vietnamese accounting terminology and standard voucher templates, but present them in an approachable way.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Responsive web app working on common browsers.
- Vietnamese UI with terminology aligned with VN accounting standards.
- High contrast for dense data tables and clear visual hierarchy for data entry forms.
